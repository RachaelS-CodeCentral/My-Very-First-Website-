PETA's origins

You know PETA was founded in 1980 by Ingrid Newkirk and Alex Pacheco, who were influenced by Australian ethicist Peter Singer's book Animal Liberation (1975). PETA's earliest efforts included exposure of and litigation against government and private research laboratories that used animals in testing.
